## 🌟 Key Features Covered

### Sales Process Management
- **Quotation Management** (Báo Giá) - RFQ to quotation conversion
- **Sales Orders** (Đơn Bán Hàng) - Order lifecycle management
- **Delivery Management** (Quản Lý Giao Hàng) - Pick, pack, ship workflows
- **Invoice Generation** (Tạo Hóa Đơn) - Automated invoice creation

### Customer Relationship Management
- **Customer Management** (Quản Lý Khách Hàng) - Complete customer lifecycle
- **Lead Management** (Quản Lý Tiềm Năng) - Lead to customer conversion
- **Opportunity Tracking** (Theo Dõi Cơ Hội) - Sales pipeline management
- **Customer Portal** (Cổng Khách Hàng) - Self-service capabilities

### Advanced Features
- **Multi-Channel Sales** (Bán Hàng Đa Kênh) - Online, offline, POS integration
- **Pricing Strategies** (Chiến Lược Giá) - Dynamic pricing, discounts, promotions
- **Commission Management** (Quản Lý Hoa Hồng) - Sales commission calculation
- **Reporting & Analytics** (Báo Cáo & Phân Tích) - Sales performance metrics
