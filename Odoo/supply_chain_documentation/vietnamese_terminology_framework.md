## 📊 Usage Guidelines

### Implementation Rules

1. **Consistency First**: Always use the standardized terminology across all documentation
2. **Context Awareness**: Choose translations that fit the specific business context
3. **Professional Language**: Use formal Vietnamese business terminology
4. **Technical Accuracy**: Maintain technical precision in translations
5. **User-Friendly**: Ensure terminology is understandable by business users
6. **International Standards**: Align with Vietnamese accounting and business standards

### Documentation Quality Standards

1. **Term Validation**: All terms must be validated with Vietnamese business standards
2. **Cross-Reference**: Maintain consistency across module boundaries
3. **Glossary Maintenance**: Keep terminology updated with Odoo version changes
4. **User Testing**: Validate terminology with actual Vietnamese users
5. **Expert Review**: Have Vietnamese business experts review terminology

### Localization Best Practices

1. **Cultural Adaptation**: Consider Vietnamese business culture in terminology
2. **Legal Compliance**: Ensure terminology complies with Vietnamese regulations
3. **Industry Standards**: Align with Vietnamese industry terminology
4. **User Acceptance**: Test terminology acceptance with target users
5. **Continuous Improvement**: Update terminology based on user feedback
